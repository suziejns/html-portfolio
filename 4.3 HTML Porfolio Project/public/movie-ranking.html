The best films according to Suzie

My top 3 films of all time

The Shawshank Redemption

This film contains some violence and sadness, but the ending is incredible. Who doesn't want to escape prison?

TopGun

The action in this film is top notch. When I first watched this film I wanted to be a fighter pilot. Sadly my eyesight is too poor to become one, but I've never lost that feeling.

Jurassic Park

Who doesn't love dinosaurs? I was obsessed with all dinosaurs as a child, my favourite is the Triceratops. This film is a tense chilling action, where you're hoping the people can survive.
